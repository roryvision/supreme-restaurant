When is Supreme Restaurant open?

We open at 11:30AM everyday and close at 10PM most days. On Fridays and Saturdays, we close at 11PM. Please follow our Instagram for the most up to date information regarding our hours.


Where is Supreme Restaurant?

You can find us at 100 Mott Street in Chinatown, NYC!


How can I make a reservation?

Please call us at (212) 966-3988 — reservations are only available for large groups of 5 or more. We also have a minimum $8 per person for dine-in.


Do you accept credit cards?

No, please come with cash or a debit card ($20 minimum).


Can I order take out?

We are on Uber Eats, Doordash, and Grubhub. You can also order at our location or over the phone, and pick up in-person.


Can I buy some soup dumplings to go?

We sell frozen soup dumplings for you to make at home. Please ask the staff for more information.


What happened to Shanghai Cafe Deluxe?

The restaurant underwent a name change after it was acquired by new management during the pandemic.
